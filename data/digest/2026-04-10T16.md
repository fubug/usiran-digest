### 核心事件

Kuwait accused Iran and its proxies of launching drone attacks on the country during the ceasefire period, while Saudi Arabia reported damage to a crucial oil pipeline in recent attacks. Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy confirmed that Ukrainian forces shot down Iranian-designed Shahed drones in multiple Middle Eastern countries.

- Kuwait's Foreign Ministry accused Iran and its proxies of launching drone attacks targeting Kuwait during the ceasefire

- Iran's Islamic Revolutionary Guard denied launching attacks on Persian Gulf states after Kuwait's announcement
- Only about 12 ships passed through the Strait of Hormuz in the first two days of the ceasefire, far below pre-war normal traffic levels
- Pakistan's capital Islamabad experienced unusual quiet as authorities locked down ahead of high-stakes talks

- Singapore's Prime Minister Lawrence Wong said the island state will not restrict fuel exports due to Iran war disruptions
