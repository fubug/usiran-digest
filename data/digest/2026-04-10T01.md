## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

80多个国家发表联合声明，强烈谴责黎巴嫩的"持续袭击"，并谴责联合国维和人员遭杀害事件。美国未签署该声明。

### 军事动态

- 黎巴嫩卫生部门报告称，周三的以色列空袭造成203人死亡，以国防部长称打击了200名"恐怖分子"
- 以色列国防部长卡茨表示，与伊朗达成的"战场分离"协议是"总理领导的重要成就"，允许以军按计划对付真主党
- 伊朗议会发言人警告，若对黎巴嫩的袭击继续，将带来"明确代价和强烈回应"
- 美国国防部称自战争爆发以来已打击超过13,000个目标

### 外交进展

- 俄罗斯外长拉夫罗夫与伊朗外长阿卜杜勒希亚通电话，欢迎美伊停火消息，强调停火协议具有地区性，包括黎巴嫩
- 德国总理默茨承认伊朗战争成为北约的"压力测试"，称以色列的战争严重程度可能导致"和平进程整体失败"
- 黎巴嫩总理纳瓦夫·萨拉姆宣布将"紧急向联合国安理会投诉"，谴责以色列"明显违反国际法原则"
- 黎巴嫩表示将向联合国投诉以色列违反国际法，指责其袭击明显违反国际人道主义法

### 分析判断

美伊停火协议执行出现严重分歧，以色列与伊朗在黎巴嫩是否属于停火范围问题上存在根本冲突。国际社会对停火能否持续持谨慎态度，联合国多国谴责以色列行动可能破坏和平进程。

## English Summary

### Core Events

More than 80 countries issued a joint statement strongly condemning "persistent attacks" in Lebanon and the killing of U.N. peacekeepers. The United States did not sign the statement.

### Military Developments

- Lebanon's Health Ministry reported 203 people killed in Israeli airstrikes Wednesday, while Israel's Defense Minister claimed to have killed "200 terrorists"
- Israel Defense Minister Katz said the "separation of arenas" agreement with Iran is an "important achievement" allowing Israel to act forcefully against Hezbollah
- Iran's Parliament Speaker warned of "explicit costs and STRONG responses" if strikes on Lebanon continue
- U.S. has struck more than 13,000 targets since the Iran war began

### Diplomatic Developments

- Russian Foreign Minister Lavrov welcomed US-Iran ceasefire and emphasized it has regional dimension including Lebanon
- German Chancellor Merz acknowledged Iran war is a "stress test" for NATO, saying Israeli actions could cause "failure of the peace process"
- Lebanon's Prime Minister Salam announced he will "urgently file a complaint with the U.N. Security Council" against Israel
- Lebanon will file complaint with U.N., accusing Israel of blatantly violating international law principles

Implementation of the US-Iran ceasefire is showing serious分歧, with Israel and Iran fundamentally divided on whether Lebanon is included in the ceasefire. The international community is cautiously optimistic about ceasefire sustainability, with multiple countries warning that Israeli actions could undermine the peace process.
