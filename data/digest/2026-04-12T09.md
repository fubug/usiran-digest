## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

- **特朗普称和平协议"无所谓"**：美国总统特朗普表示无论是否达成协议美国都已"获胜"，称"是否达成协议对我来说无所谓"，因为"我们已经战胜了他们军事上"。
- **教皇Leo XIV谴责战争**：教皇在梵蒂冈和平祈祷会上强烈谴责"战争狂妄"，称"够了！够了战争！"，指责这种"全能的幻觉"正在变得"越来越不可预测和具有攻击性"。
- **美伊谈判继续僵持**：在伊斯兰堡的高层会谈已持续超过15小时，进入第二天凌晨，双方在霍尔木兹海峡控制权问题上存在"严重分歧"，谈判尚未取得突破性进展。

### 军事动态

- 以色列继续在黎巴嫩南部进行空袭，但未袭击贝鲁特
- 美军宣称两艘驱逐舰穿越霍尔木兹海峡进行水雷清理工作，伊朗否认这一说法
- 黎巴嫩卫生部称新一轮冲突已造成2,020人死亡，其中357人在以色列周三的袭击中死亡
- 美国中央司令部表示将部署包括水下无人机在内的更多部队参与海峡清理行动

### 外交进展

- **伊斯兰堡马拉松谈判**：美国副总统万斯率领的代表团与伊朗议会 speaker Ghalibaf率领的代表团在巴基斯坦继续会谈，已超过15小时
- **代表团成员**：美方正副总统万斯、特使威特科夫、女婿库什纳；伊朗外长阿拉奇等高级官员参与
- **国际斡旋**：中国、埃及、沙特、卡塔尔官员在伊斯兰堡间接促进谈判
- **以色列-黎巴嫩**：两国驻美大使预计下周在华盛顿举行直接会谈，但立即结束黎巴嫩战争的前景不乐观

### 分析判断

特朗普的无所谓表态显示美国对谈判结果信心十足，但也反映了谈判的僵局。教皇的强硬谴责增加了国际社会对战争的道德压力。美伊双方在霍尔木兹海峡控制权问题上的分歧是主要障碍，伊朗寻求对战略水道的控制权，而美国则要求立即开放。谈判持续超15小时表明双方仍在努力寻找共同点，但突破前景不明朗。

## English Summary

### Core Events

- **Trump: Peace Deal 'Makes No Difference'**: US President Donald Trump stated it "makes no difference" whether a peace deal is reached, declaring "We've defeated them militarily... Whether we make a deal or not, it makes no difference to me."
- **Pope Leo XIV Condemns War**: The Pope issued a strong rebuke to the "delusion of omnipotence" fueling the conflict, declaring "Enough of war!" during a peace vigil in Vatican City.
- **US-Iran Talks Stalemate**: High-level talks in Islamabad have stretched beyond 15 hours into early Sunday morning, with "serious disagreements" remaining, particularly over control of the Strait of Hormuz.

### Military Developments

- Israel continues airstrikes in southern Lebanon but has not struck Beirut since Wednesday
- US claims two destroyers transited Strait of Hormuz for mine-clearing operations, which Iran denies
- Lebanon health ministry reports 2,020 deaths in the latest fighting, including 357 from Israeli strikes on Wednesday
- US Central Command states additional forces, including underwater drones, will join clearance efforts

### Diplomatic Developments

- **Marathon Islamabad Talks**: US delegation led by VP Vance continues discussions with Iranian delegation led by Parliament Speaker Ghalibaf beyond 15 hours
- **Delegation Members**: US side includes VP Vance, envoy Witkoff, Kushner; Iranian side includes Foreign Minister Araghchi and other senior officials
- **International Mediation**: Chinese, Egyptian, Saudi, and Qatari officials are in Islamabad to indirectly facilitate talks
- **Israel-Lebanon**: Both countries' ambassadors to US expected to meet in Washington next week, but immediate end to Lebanon war unlikely

### Analysis

Trump's indifferent stance reflects US confidence in military victory but also indicates negotiation impasse. The Pope's strong condemnation increases international moral pressure on the conflict. The main sticking point remains control of the Strait of Hormuz, with Iran seeking strategic control and the US demanding immediate reopening. The 15+ hour duration suggests both sides are still seeking common ground, but breakthrough prospects remain uncertain.
