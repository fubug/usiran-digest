## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

国际货币基金组织（IMF）总裁格奥尔基耶娃警告，伊朗战争正在拖累全球经济增长前景，IMF将在下周下调全球经济增长预测。

### 军事动态

- 伊朗战争自2月28日开始以来，已对全球经济造成冲击
- 以色列和黎巴嫩真主党冲突持续，黎巴嫩报告在以色列空袭后出现"最致命的一天"
- 伊朗关闭霍尔木兹海峡，影响全球能源运输

### 外交进展

- 欧洲领导人呼吁通过谈判解决伊朗危机
- 美国和伊朗宣布达成为期两周的停火协议，但协议的持久性存疑
- IMF警告即使新的和平协议能够持久，经济增长仍将放缓

### 关键数据

| 指标 | 数据 | 来源 |
|------|------|------|
| 全球经济增长预测 | 3.3%（原预测值） | IMF |
| 原油和天然气价格上涨 | 受冲突影响 | IMF |
| 肥料供应链中断 | 影响全球农民 | IMF |
| 能源冲击影响地区 | 撒哈拉以南非洲和小岛国家最脆弱 | IMF |

### 分析判断

IMF的警告表明伊朗战争对全球经济的影响正在加深，即使停火协议能够维持，经济增长前景依然黯淡。各国政策制定者需要谨慎应对，避免采取可能加剧危机的单边行动。

## English Summary

### Core Events

International Monetary Fund (IMF) Managing Director Kristalina Georgieva warned that the Iran war is darkening the global economic outlook, with the fund set to downgrade its global growth forecast next week.

### Military Developments

- The Iran war, which began Feb. 28, has delivered a shock to the global economy
- Israel-Hezbollah conflict continues, with Lebanon reporting "deadliest day" following Israeli airstrikes
- Iran has closed the Strait of Hormuz, affecting global energy transportation

### Diplomatic Developments

- European leaders urge negotiated settlement to Iran crisis
- US and Iran announced a 2-week ceasefire, but its durability is questionable
- IMF warns that even if the new peace is durable, growth will be slower

### Key Data

| Metric | Data | Source |
|--------|------|--------|
| Global growth forecast | 3.3% (original forecast) | IMF |
| Oil and gas price increases | Affected by conflict | IMF |
| Fertilizer supply chain disruptions | Impacting global farmers | IMF |
| Energy shock impact regions | Sub-Saharan Africa and small island countries most vulnerable | IMF |

### Analysis

The IMF's warning indicates that the economic impact of the Iran war is deepening, with even a sustained ceasefire likely resulting in slower global growth. Policymakers must be cautious to avoid unilateral actions that could exacerbate the crisis.
