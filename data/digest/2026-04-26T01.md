## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

特朗普总统突然宣布取消特使威特科夫和库什纳前往巴基斯坦与伊朗进行和平谈判的行程，称"没有人知道谁在负责"。伊朗外长阿卜杜拉希奇在巴基斯坦停留约20小时后已离开，预计周日至周一返回。

### 军事动态

- 以色列军方称在黎巴嫩南部多地发动空袭，击毙6名"武装分子"
- 黎巴嫩真主党向以色列发射火箭弹和无人机，未造成伤亡
- 以色列拦截1枚"可疑空中目标"，但未进入以色列领空
- 黎巴嫩卫生部门将死亡人数上调至2,496人，受伤人数7,725人

### 外交进展

- 黎巴嫩逊尼派最高宗教权威支持总统与以色列谈判的权利
- 伊朗外长阿卜杜拉希奇与巴基斯坦总理谢巴兹·谢里夫举行会谈
- 伊朗表示将通过巴基斯坦作为"桥梁"传达和平提案，拒绝与美国直接会谈
- 特朗普称伊朗"内部争斗和混乱"，美方"持有所有王牌"

### 分析判断

特朗普取消特使行程表明美国对当前谈判进程的不满，可能是在向伊朗施压。黎巴嫩战场虽有停火协议，但双方仍小规模交火，局势依然脆弱。伊朗能源基础设施受损可能带来长期影响，伊朗呼吁民众节约用电显示战争对民生的影响已显现。

## English Summary

### Core Events

President Trump abruptly canceled the trip of envoys Steve Witkoff and Jared Kushner to Pakistan for Iran peace talks, stating "Nobody knows who is in charge." Iranian Foreign Minister Abbas Araghchi departed Pakistan after about 20 hours of talks, expected to return Sunday or Monday.

### Military Developments

- Israeli military conducted strikes in southern Lebanon, killing 6 "militants" according to IDF
- Hezbollah launched rockets and drones at Israel, causing no injuries
- Israel intercepted 1 "suspicious aerial target" that did not cross into Israeli territory
- Lebanese Health Ministry raised death toll to 2,496 with 7,725 wounded

### Diplomatic Developments

- Lebanon's Sunni religious authority supports president's right to negotiate with Israel
- Iranian Foreign Minister met with Pakistani Prime Minister Shehbaz Sharif
- Iran said it will use Pakistan as "bridge" to convey peace proposals, refusing direct US talks
- Trump called Iran's leadership "tremendous infighting and confusion," saying US "has all the cards"

### Analysis

Trump's cancellation of envoy trip indicates US dissatisfaction with current negotiation process, likely signaling pressure on Iran. Despite ceasefire agreements, both sides continue low-level exchanges in Lebanon, showing fragile stability. Iranian energy infrastructure damage may have long-term effects, with the government urging electricity conservation indicating war's impact on civilian life is becoming apparent.
