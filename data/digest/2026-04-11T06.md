## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

**黎巴嫩与以色列将于周二在华盛顿举行停火谈判**，标志着地区紧张局势可能迎来重要转折点。黎巴嫩总统府确认，黎以双方同意下周二在美国国务院举行会谈，讨论停火和启动直接谈判，谈判在美国斡旋下进行。

**伊朗高级代表团抵达伊斯兰堡**参加和平谈判，由伊朗议会发言人穆罕默德·巴赫尔·加利巴夫和外交部长阿卜杜勒·阿巴斯·阿拉格奇率领的高级别代表团已抵达巴基斯坦首都。

### 军事动态

- 以色列军方称，两天前在黎巴嫩实施的空袭击杀了超过180名真主党武装分子
- 黎巴嫩卫生部统计，以军两天前在全国范围的空袭造成357人死亡（从之前的303人死亡上升），1223人受伤
- 周三是以色列-真主党冲突五周多来最致命的一天
- 据统计，已有1953人在冲突中丧生，其中包括102名妇女、130名儿童和57名急救人员
- 冲突导致超过100万人流离失所，6300多人受伤

### 外交进展

- **Pope Leo XIV公开谴责伊朗战争**，称"上帝不会祝福任何冲突"，特别是不站在"昨天挥剑、今天投弹的人"一边
- **特朗普强硬表态**，称伊朗人似乎没有意识到"除了使用国际水道进行短期勒索外，他们没有任何筹码"，声称"他们今天还活着唯一的原因是为了谈判！"
- 特朗普威胁如果谈判不成将继续进行大规模军事打击，称海军舰艇正在装载"最好的弹药"
- **巴勒斯坦权力机构主席阿巴斯·马哈茂德·法塔赫称**，乌克兰军队在伊朗战争期间在多个中东国家击落了沙赫德无人机

### 分析判断

## English Summary

### Core Events

**Lebanon and Israel to hold ceasefire talks in Washington on Tuesday**, marking a potential significant turning point in regional tensions. Lebanon's presidency confirmed that both sides agreed to meet next Tuesday at the State Department to discuss declaring a ceasefire and launching direct negotiations under US auspices.

**High-level Iranian delegation arrives in Islamabad** for peace talks, with a delegation led by Iranian Parliament Speaker Mohammad Bagher Ghalibaf and Foreign Minister Abbas Araghchi arriving in the Pakistani capital.

### Military Developments

- Israeli military claims airstrikes in Lebanon two days ago killed over 180 Hezbollah militants
- Lebanon's Health Ministry reports the death toll from Israeli strikes nationwide rose to 357 (up from previous 303), with 1,223 more wounded
- Wednesday was the deadliest day in over five weeks of renewed Israel-Hezbollah fighting
- Total casualties: 1,953 killed (including 102 women, 130 children, and 57 paramedics)
- Over 1 million people displaced, 6,300+ injured

### Diplomatic Developments

- **Pope Leo XIV publicly condemns Iran war**, stating "God does not bless any conflict" and "never stands on the side of those who yesterday wielded the sword and today drop bombs"
- **Trump makes strong statements**, claiming Iranians "seem to realize they have no cards, other than a short term extortion of the World by using International Waterways" and "The only reason they are alive today is to negotiate!"
- Trump threatens continued military action if talks fail, stating U.S. Navy ships are being reloaded with "the best ammunition"
- **Palestinian Authority Authority Chairman Mahmoud Abbas** says Ukrainian forces shot down Shahed drones in Middle Eastern countries during Iran war
