## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

美伊巴三方在伊斯兰堡的历史性和平谈判持续超过9小时，从当地时间下午5点开始，已延伸至周日凌晨，仍在继续中。与此同时，美国海军两艘驱逐舰开始执行霍尔木兹海峡水雷清理任务，教皇利奥十四世举行和平祈祷活动呼吁停止战争。

### 军事动态

- **霍尔木兹水雷清理**: 美国海军"弗兰克·E·彼得森"号和"迈克尔·墨菲"号两艘驱逐舰已进入霍尔木兹海峡，开始清理水雷行动。CENTCOM指挥官布拉德·库珀表示将建立新的安全航道，并与海运行业共享。
- **以色列军事行动**: 以色列总理内塔尼亚胡声明对伊朗的军事行动"尚未结束"，称"我们打击了他们，但还有更多要做"。以色列军队持续在黎巴嫩打击真主党目标。
- **美国资产政策**: 美国官员确认伊朗被冻结的资产不会解除，伊朗谈判代表加里巴夫此前释放资产的要求被拒绝。

### 外交进展

- **伊斯兰堡和谈进展**: 万斯率领的美国代表团与伊朗议会加里巴夫及外长阿拉格奇进行面对面会谈，巴基斯坦总理谢巴兹·谢里夫分别与美国和伊朗代表团会面
- **教皇和平呼吁**: 教皇利奥十四世举行和平祈祷，呼吁世界领导人选择对话而非战争，要求"坐在谈判和调解的桌边，而不是制定军备和致命行动的桌边"
- **黎以华盛顿会谈**: 以色列和黎巴嫩外交官将在华盛顿特区举行停火谈判，黎巴嫩总统约瑟夫·奥恩宣布周二会谈

## English Summary

### Core Events

Historic trilateral peace talks between the U.S., Iran, and Pakistan in Islamabad have extended beyond 9 hours, continuing from 5 p.m. local time into early Sunday morning. Concurrently, U.S. Navy destroyers initiated mine-clearing operations in the Strait of Hormuz, while Pope Leo XIV held a peace vigil calling for an end to the conflict.

### Military Developments

- **Hormuz Mine Clearance**: Two U.S. Navy destroyers, USS Frank E. Peterson and USS Michael Murphy, entered the Strait of Hormuz to begin clearing naval mines. Adm. Brad Cooper confirmed establishing a new safe passage for maritime commerce.
- **Israeli Campaign Continues**: Prime Minister Netanyahu stated Israel's military campaign against Iran "is not over," declaring "We hit them, we still have more to do." Israeli forces continue targeting Hezbollah in Lebanon.
- **US Asset Policy**: U.S. officials confirmed frozen Iranian assets will not be released, contradicting demands from Iranian negotiator Ghalibaf for asset unfreezing before talks progress.

### Diplomatic Developments

- **Islamabad Talks Progress**: U.S. delegation led by VP Vance continues face-to-face discussions with Iranian Parliament Speaker Ghalibaf and Foreign Minister Araghchi. Pakistan Prime Minister Shehbaz Sharif met separately with both delegations.
- **Pope's Peace Appeal**: Pope Leo XIV held a peace vigil, urging world leaders to "choose dialogue over war" and reject conflict "not only in word, but in deed."
- **Lebanon-Israel Talks**: Diplomats from Israel and Lebanon will meet in Washington D.C. for ceasefire negotiations, as announced by Lebanon's President Joseph Aoun.
