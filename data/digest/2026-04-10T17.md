## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

美伊伊斯兰堡会谈即将启动。法国、西班牙等多国密集外交斡旋；伊朗宣布所有银行将于周六全面恢复运营，释放经济回暖信号。特朗普接受 CBS 采访时称已建议内塔尼亚胡减少对黎巴嫩的打击，并预言将达成"非常成功的协议"。

### 军事动态

- 伊朗伊斯兰革命卫队正式否认在停火期间对任何波斯湾国家发动导弹或无人机袭击，称相关报道为"未经证实的谣言"
- 迪拜将外国航空公司航班限制延长至5月31日，印度航空公司受冲击最大（因同时被禁飞巴基斯坦领空）
- 日本释放额外20天石油储备以应对供应中断

### 外交进展

- **伊斯兰堡会谈**：美方代表团包括副总统万斯、特使 Witkoff、库什纳；伊方代表团包括外长阿拉格齐和议长加利巴夫。代表团级讨论今日开始，核心谈判阶段安排在周六
- 法国外长巴罗与巴基斯坦副总理达尔通话，祝贺巴基斯坦促成停火，支持其继续推动和平
- 西班牙外长与伊朗外长通话，敦促伊方"以善意参与谈判"，并要求停止所有导弹和无人机发射
- 巴基斯坦前外长比拉瓦尔表示，解除对伊制裁将惠及巴基斯坦和全球
- 巴基斯坦外交部为参会记者和代表提供落地签证
- 伊斯兰堡红区全面封锁，内政部设立联合控制室统筹安保

### 分析判断

伊斯兰堡会谈是停火协议能否转化为持久和平的关键节点。美方派出万斯+库什纳+特使的豪华阵容，显示特朗普高度重视此次谈判。伊方代表团包括议长加利巴夫，暗示伊朗议会派也在施加影响。多国密集外交活动表明国际社会对停火脆弱性有清醒认识。伊朗银行恢复运营是积极信号，暗示德黑兰内部对谈判前景持谨慎乐观态度。

## English Summary

### Core Events

US-Iran Islamabad talks are set to begin. France and Spain engaged in intensive diplomatic mediation; Iran announced all banks will fully resume operations Saturday, signaling economic normalization. President Trump told CBS he has advised Netanyahu to scale back Lebanon strikes and predicted a "very successful agreement."

### Military Developments

- Iran's IRGC officially denied launching any missile or drone strikes on Persian Gulf states during the ceasefire, calling reports "unverified rumors"
- Dubai extended foreign airline restrictions to one flight per day per carrier until May 31, with Indian airlines hit hardest
- Japan released additional 20 days of oil reserves to address supply disruptions

### Diplomatic Developments

- **Islamabad Talks**: US delegation includes VP Vance, envoy Witkoff, and Kushner; Iran delegation includes FM Araghchi and Parliament Speaker Ghalibaf. Delegation-level talks begin today, core negotiations set for Saturday
- French FM Barrot called Pakistan's DPM Dar, congratulating Pakistan on brokering ceasefire
- Spanish FM urged Iran to "negotiate in good faith" and halt all missile/drone launches in call with Iranian counterpart
- Pakistan's former FM Bilawal Bhutto Zardari said lifting Iran sanctions would benefit Pakistan and the world
- Pakistan offers visa-on-arrival for journalists and delegates attending talks
- Islamabad's Red Zone completely sealed, joint control room established at Interior Ministry

### Analysis

The Islamabad talks represent the critical juncture where the fragile ceasefire could transition into lasting peace. The US delegation's senior composition (VP + Kushner + envoy) signals Trump's high stakes in the negotiation. Iran including Parliament Speaker Ghalibaf suggests domestic political factions are exerting influence.
