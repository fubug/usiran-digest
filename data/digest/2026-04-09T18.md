## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

特朗普在中东增兵并威胁伊朗，称不遵守"真正"停火协议将采取行动。以色列明确表示黎巴嫩不在停火协议范围内，导致地区紧张局势持续升级。

### 新进展 (18:00-19:00)

- **美军增兵**: 特朗普宣布向中东增派军事资产，等待与伊朗达成"真正协议"
- **外交表态**: 海湾国家对美伊协议表示担忧，担心伊朗对霍尔木兹海峡影响力
- **停火范围**: 美国澄清停火协议不包括对黎巴嫩真主党作战

### 背景 (17:00前事件)
- 以色列空袭黎巴嫩造成182死→254死，黎巴嫩宣布全国哀悼日
- 特朗普批评北约在伊朗战争中"未能及时提供支持"
- 巴基斯坦继续斡旋美伊双方维护停火协议完整性

### 分析判断

增兵威胁显示美国试图在停火与施压间寻求平衡，但实际执行面临挑战。海湾国家的担忧反映地区对美伊关系变化的谨慎态度，停火协议的完整性面临考验。

## English Summary

### Core Events

Trump reinforced troops in the Middle East and threatened Iran, stating action will be taken if a "real" ceasefire is not complied with. Israel clarified that Lebanon is excluded from the ceasefire agreement, leading to continued regional escalation.

### New Developments (18:00-19:00)

- **US Reinforcements**: Trump announced additional military assets to the Middle East, awaiting a "real deal" with Iran
- **Diplomatic Statements**: Gulf states expressed concerns about US-Iran agreement, fearing Iranian influence over Hormuz Strait
- **Ceasefire Scope**: US clarified that the ceasefire does not include operations against Hezbollah in Lebanon

### Background (pre-17:00 events)
- Israeli airstrikes in Lebanon caused 182 dead → 254 dead, Lebanon declared national mourning
- Trump criticized NATO for "not providing timely support" during the Iran war
- Pakistan continued mediating between US and Iran to maintain ceasefire integrity

### Analysis

The reinforcement threat shows the US attempting to balance between ceasefire and pressure, but implementation faces challenges. Gulf states' concerns reflect regional caution about changing US-Iran relations, with the ceasefire agreement's integrity under pressure.
