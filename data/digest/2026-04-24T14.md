## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

美伊在霍尔木兹海峡的对峙急剧升级，油价突破106美元/桶，双方互相关押船只，该关键水道商业航运几乎完全中断。

### 军事动态

- 美国海军扣押了一艘装载伊朗原油的油轮
- 伊斯兰革命卫队在霍尔木兹海峡扣留了两艘外国货轮（巴拿马籍MSC Francesca和希腊籍Epaminondas）
- 特朗普下令美国海军控制霍尔木兹海峡所有船只通行
- 4月23日仅9艘商业船只通过海峡（正常为每天129艘）

### 外交进展

- 特朗普在Truth Social上发布强硬声明，宣布霍尔木兹海峡"完全封锁"
- 伊朗伊斯兰革命卫队指控外国船只"危及海上安全并篡改导航系统"
- 希腊政府否认Epaminondas号被扣留，称船长仍控制船只

### 分析判断

美伊在霍尔木兹海峡的对峙已经从局部冲突升级为全面封锁，直接威胁全球能源供应安全。特朗普的"完全封锁"声明标志着美国已经超出了伊朗港口封锁范围，可能触发更大规模的地缘政治危机。

## English Summary

### Core Events

US-Iran standoff in Strait of Hormuz has escalated dramatically, with oil prices surging above $106 per barrel as both sides mutually detain vessels and commercial shipping through the critical waterway has nearly completely halted.

### Military Developments

- US Navy seized a tanker carrying Iranian oil
- Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps captured two foreign cargo ships in Strait of Hormuz (Panama-flagged MSC Francesca and Greek-owned Epaminondas)
- Trump ordered US Navy to control all vessel transit through Strait of Hormuz
- Only 9 commercial vessels transited the strait on April 23 (down from normal 129)

### Diplomatic Developments

- Trump posted强硬声明 on Truth Social declaring Strait of Hormuz "completely sealed"
- Iran's IRGC accused foreign vessels of "endangering maritime security and tampering with navigation systems"
- Greek government denied Epaminondas was captured, stating the vessel remains under captain's control

### Analysis

The US-Iran standoff in Strait of Hormuz has escalated from localized conflict to comprehensive blockade, directly threatening global energy supply security. Trump's "completely sealed" declaration indicates the US has expanded its naval blockade beyond Iranian ports, potentially triggering a larger-scale geopolitical crisis.
