## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

美伊巴伊斯兰堡三方高层直接谈判持续超过9小时，美国开始清理霍尔木兹海峡水雷，以色列声称24小时内打击黎巴嫩200个目标。

### 军事动态

- 美国海军驱逐舰 USS Frank E. Peterson 和 USS Michael Murphy 通过霍尔木兹海峡，开始清理伊朗布设的水雷作业
- 伊朗伊斯兰革命卫队警告将"严厉处置"通过霍尔木兹海峡的军事舰艇，只允许民用船只"在特定条件下"通过
- 以色列国防军声称在24小时内打击黎巴嫩超过200个真主党目标，包括基础设施和发射器
- 美国中央司令部宣布更多水下无人机将加入海峡清理作业

### 外交进展

- 美国副总统万斯、总统女婿库什纳和特使威特科夫与伊朗议会发言人加里巴夫、外长阿拉格奇及巴基斯坦官员在伊斯兰堡举行三方面对面会谈
- 会谈已持续超过9小时，从当地时间周六下午5点开始，目前仍在进行中（当地时间周日凌晨2点）
- 联合国多个机构负责人联合声明要求追究中东地区违反国际法责任
- 特朗普在社交媒体上宣布霍尔木兹海峡即将重开，称"空船只正涌向美国装载货物"

## English Summary

### Core Events

US-Iran-Pakistan trilateral face-to-face talks in Islamabad continue beyond 9 hours, US Navy begins mine-clearing operations in Strait of Hormuz, Israel claims striking over 200 targets in Lebanon within 24 hours.

### Military Developments

- USS Frank E. Peterson and USS Michael Murphy destroyers transited Strait of Hormuz, beginning mine-clearing operations from Iranian-laid mines
- Iran's Revolutionary Guards threatened to deal "severely" with military vessels transiting the strait, allowing only civilian vessels "under specific conditions"
- Israel Defense Forces claimed to have struck over 200 Hezbollah targets in Lebanon within 24 hours, including infrastructure and launchers
- CENTCOM announced additional US underwater drones will join the clearance effort in coming days

### Diplomatic Developments

- US Vice President JD Vance, President Trump's son-in-law Jared Kushner, and Special Envoy Steve Witkoff met with Iranian Parliament Speaker Mohammad-Bagher Ghalibaf, Foreign Minister Abbas Araghchi, and Pakistani officials
- Talks have continued for over 9 hours, starting Saturday 5 PM local time and ongoing as of Sunday 2 AM local
- Multiple UN agency heads issued joint statement demanding accountability for international law violations in the Middle East
- President Trump announced via social media that Strait of Hormuz will "soon be open," with "empty ships rushing to the United States to 'load up'"
