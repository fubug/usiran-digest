## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

黎巴嫩遭遇战争爆发以来单日死亡人数最多的一天，以色列空袭造成至少203人死亡。停火协议因双方对协议范围的理解分歧而持续紧张，美军在中东保持全面战备状态。

### 军事动态

- **黎巴嫩重大伤亡**: 以色列空袭贝鲁特和其他地区造成至少203人死亡，超过1000人受伤，创战争以来单日最高死亡记录
- **真主党回应**: 黎巴嫩真主党宣布向以色列北部发射火箭弹，袭击了Manara地区
- **以军持续行动**: 以色列总理内塔尼亚胡宣布将继续"用力量、精确和决心"打击真主党，称已消灭真主党领导人哈桑·纳斯拉姆的秘书阿里·优素福·哈尔希
- **美军战备**: 特朗普宣布所有美国军事资产、人员、武器装备将保持在中东"直到真正协议完全执行"

### 外交进展

- **巴勒斯坦斡旋**: 巴基斯坦确认谈判将于周五在伊斯兰堡开始，美国副总统万斯将率团参加
- **联合国警告**: 联合国秘书长警告以色列在黎巴嫩的军事活动对"停火构成严重风险"
- **法国行动**: 法国宣布正在与参与国家 finalize 在霍尔木兹海峡护送船只的计划，预计约15国参与
- **西班牙外交**: 西班牙宣布将在重新点燃和平希望期间重新开放驻德黑兰大使馆

## English Summary

### Core Events

Lebanon experienced its deadliest day since the war began, with Israeli airstrikes causing at least 203 deaths. The ceasefire agreement remains strained due to disagreements over its scope, as US forces maintain full combat readiness in the Middle East.

### Military Developments

- **Lebanon Mass Casualties**: Israeli airstrikes on Beirut and other areas caused at least 203 deaths and over 1,000 injuries, marking the highest single-day death toll since the war began
- **Hezbollah Response**: Lebanese Hezbollah announced rocket attacks on northern Israel's Manara area
- **Israeli Continued Operations**: Prime Minister Netanyahu declared Israel will continue "striking Hezbollah with force, precision, and determination," claiming to have killed Ali Yusuf Harshi, secretary to Hezbollah leader Naim Qassem
- **US Combat Readiness**: Trump announced all US military assets, personnel, and equipment will remain in the Middle East "until a REAL AGREEMENT is fully complied with"

### Diplomatic Developments

- **Pakistan Mediation**: Pakistan confirmed negotiations will begin in Islamabad on Friday, with US Vice President JD Vance leading the US delegation
- **UN Warning**: UN Secretary-General warned that Israeli military activity in Lebanon "poses a grave risk" to the fragile ceasefire
- **French Action**: France announced finalizing plans for a mission to escort ships in the Strait of Hormuz with about 15 participating countries
- **Spanish Diplomacy**: Spain announced it will reopen its embassy in Tehran during this "hope for peace is rekindled"
