## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

**Deadline 到期（美东4月7日晚8点 / 北京4月8日早8点）**

特朗普设定的伊朗重开霍尔木兹海峡最后期限已到期。在此之前，双方通过巴基斯坦、埃及、土耳其调停方进行了密集谈判，但未能达成协议。

### 军事动态

- **伊朗拒绝停火提案**：伊朗正式拒绝巴基斯坦调停方提出的45天临时停火方案，坚持要求永久解决方案及安全保证。伊朗驻开罗外交代表团负责人表示："我们不会仅仅接受停火，我们只接受战争的永久结束。"
- **伊朗反提10项条件**：通过巴基斯坦调停方递交，特朗普回应称"not good enough, but a significant step"
- **以色列空袭德黑兰**：打击革命卫队据点、导弹基地、石化工厂，持续数小时
- **击杀革命卫队情报局长**：Majid Khademi 在以军空袭中被击杀
- **以色列攻击南帕尔斯天然气田**：伊朗最大石化基地再度遭袭
- **伊朗导弹袭击海法**：4名以色列平民死亡，救援人员正从废墟中清理遗体
- **伊朗凌晨新一轮反击**：向阿联酋、沙特、巴林发射导弹和无人机
  - 沙特拦截7枚弹道导弹
  - 阿联酋防空系统启动拦截
  - 巴林再次拉响警报
- **德黑兰居民区遭空袭**：伊朗红新月会证实，救援人员已赶赴现场
- **谢里夫理工大学遭空袭**：伊朗顶尖理工学府被击中（该大学受西方制裁，因与伊朗军方和武器研发机构有关联）
- **伊拉克埃尔比勒遭无人机袭击**：2人死亡，伊朗被指为袭击方
- **科威特阿里萨利姆空军基地遭无人机袭击**：15名美军受伤
- **CENTCOM数据更新**：美军已打击超过13,000个目标，155+艘伊朗船只被毁/损坏

### 外交进展

- **多方调停**：土耳其、埃及、巴基斯坦组成调停小组积极斡旋
- **谈判参与者**：特朗普、JD Vance、鲁比奥、Witkoff、库什纳参与美方谈判团队
- **联合国安理会即将投票**：巴林提出的保护霍尔木兹海峡商船决议案
- **特朗普称伊朗"正在善意谈判"**，但同时坚持要求"自由通航石油及一切物资"

## English Summary

### Core Events

**Deadline Expired (April 7, 8:00 PM ET / April 8, 8:00 AM Beijing Time)**

Trump's deadline for Iran to reopen the Strait of Hormuz has passed. Intensive negotiations through Pakistani, Egyptian, and Turkish mediators failed to produce an agreement before the deadline.

### Military Developments

- **Iran rejects ceasefire proposal**: Iran formally rejected the 45-day temporary ceasefire brokered by Pakistani mediators, demanding a permanent end to the war with security guarantees. Head of Iranian diplomatic mission in Cairo: "We won't merely accept a ceasefire. We only accept a permanent end to the war."
- **Iran counters with 10-point proposal**: Submitted through Pakistani mediators. Trump called it "not good enough, but a significant step."
- **Israeli airstrikes on Tehran**: Extensive strikes targeting IRGC sites, missile bases, and petrochemical plants lasting several hours
- **IRGC intelligence chief killed**: Majid Khademi eliminated in Israeli airstrike
- **South Pars gas field attacked**: Iran's largest petrochemical complex struck again by Israel
- **Iranian missile strike on Haifa**: 4 Israeli civilians killed
- **Iran's overnight counter-attacks**: Launched missiles and drones at UAE, Saudi Arabia, and Bahrain
  - Saudi Arabia intercepted 7 ballistic missiles
  - UAE air defenses engaged
  - Bahrain sirens activated for the second time
- **Tehran residential area hit**: Confirmed by Iranian Red Crescent Society, relief workers dispatched
- **Sharif University of Technology bombed**: Iran's top science/engineering institution (under Western sanctions for military ties)
- **Drone strike on Irbil, Iraq**: 2 killed, attributed to Iran
- **Drone strike on Ali Al Salem Air Base, Kuwait**: 15 US service members injured
- **CENTCOM update**: 13,000+ targets struck, 155+ Iranian vessels damaged/destroyed

### Diplomatic Developments

- **Multi-party mediation**: Turkey, Egypt, Pakistan forming mediation group
- **US negotiation team**: Trump, JD Vance, Rubio, Witkoff, Kushner involved
- **UN Security Council vote upcoming**: Bahrain's draft resolution to protect commercial shipping through Strait of Hormuz
- **Trump claims Iran "negotiating in good faith"** while insisting on "free traffic of oil and everything else"

- Deadline passed but Trump has not immediately executed threatened strikes; negotiation window remains open

- Iran's overnight multi-country missile launches represent a "attack as defense" strategy
