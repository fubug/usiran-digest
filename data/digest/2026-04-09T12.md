## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

### 军事动态

- 特朗普指示所有美国军事人员、装备继续留驻中东，"直至真正协议完全遵守"
- 伊朗宣布霍尔木兹海峡替代航线，要求船只避开雷区使用新航道
- Hezbollah向以色列北部Manara发射火箭，称"持续反击直至以色列 aggression 停止"

- 联合国秘书长古特雷斯警告黎巴嫩军事行动对"脆弱美伊停火构成严重风险"
- 法国总统马克龙与特朗普、伊朗总统佩什基扬通话，呼吁停火全面适用于所有冲突区域
- 美国副总统万斯澄清停火不包括黎巴嫩，称这是"合理误解"
- 阿联酋要求伊朗赔偿海湾邻国损失，寻求确保德黑兰停止敌对行为

## English Summary

### Core Events

President Trump ordered all U.S. military assets to remain in the Middle East, the UN warned that Israeli actions in Lebanon pose "grave risk" to the US-Iran ceasefire, putting the fragile truce under severe pressure.

### Military Developments

- Trump orders all U.S. military personnel and hardware to remain in the Middle East "until such time as the REAL AGREEMENT reached is fully complied with"
- Lebanon's health ministry reported Israeli airstrikes on Wednesday killed 182 people and wounded 890, the highest single-day toll in the Israel-Hezbollah war
- Iran announced alternative routes through Strait of Hormuz, instructing ships to avoid mines using new passages
- Hezbollah rockets fired at northern Israel's Manara, promising "response will continue until Israeli-American aggression ceases"

### Diplomatic Developments

- UN Secretary-General Guterres warned Lebanese military activity poses "grave risk to the fragile US-Iran truce"
- French President Macron spoke with both Trump and Iranian President Pezeshkian, urging ceasefire be fully respected across all confrontation areas
- U.S. Vice President Vance clarified ceasefire doesn't include Lebanon, calling it a "reasonable misunderstanding"
- UAE demanded Iran pay for damages to Gulf neighbors, seeking assurances Tehran will cease hostilities

### Analysis

The US-Iran ceasefire is showing clear signs of strain, with large-scale Israeli airstrikes in Lebanon and Iran's fierce reaction casting serious doubt on the truce's future.
