## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

**Deadline 倒计时（美东4月7日晚8点 / 北京4月8日早8点）**

特朗普在最新声明中警告："如果今晚达不成协议，整个文明将消亡，再也无法恢复。" 同时表示"也许会发生革命性的好事"，保留了谈判余地。空袭持续升级，伊朗两座桥梁和一座火车站被击中。

### 军事动态

- **空袭伊朗桥梁和火车站**：铁路桥、火车站、公路桥遭空袭，美以未立即认责（AP News）
- **美军再次打击哈尔克岛（Kharg Island）**：白宫官员证实美军打击了伊朗石油枢纽哈尔克岛的军事目标，这是该岛第二次遭袭（AP News）
- **以色列连续第二天空袭设拉子石化设施**：以色列称打击目标为用于制造炸药的石化厂
- **伊朗向沙特发射7枚弹道导弹**：碎片散落在能源设施附近，被拦截
- **伊朗向以色列发射导弹**：特拉维夫和埃拉特报告有导弹来袭
- **革命卫队威胁**：称如果特朗普执行威胁，伊朗将"切断美国及其盟友在该地区数年的石油和天然气供应"，并扩大对海湾地区的攻击
- **阿尔伯兹省空袭致18人死亡**，其他地区另有15人死亡（伊朗国家媒体）
- **库尔德拉巴德国际机场遭打击**（伊朗西部）

### 外交进展

- **伊朗总统称1400万人志愿参战**：Masoud Pezeshkian 在X上发文称已响应国家媒体和短信征召，并称自己也将加入
- **革命卫队将领呼吁家长让孩子去检查站**：paramilitary Revolutionary Guard 将领要求父母送子女去前线
- **法国外长警告战争罪**：Jean-Noël Barrot 称攻击民用和能源基础设施"被战争法规和国际法所禁止"，将引发新的升级恶性循环
- **联合国秘书长警告美国**：António Guterres 警告攻击民用基础设施违反国际法
- **特朗普：对战争罪"完全不担心"**
- **谈判仍在进行**：但伊朗已拒绝美方最新提案，能否在 deadline 前达成协议不明

## English Summary

### Core Events

**Deadline Countdown (April 7, 8:00 PM ET / April 8, 8:00 AM Beijing)**

Trump warned in his latest statement: "A whole civilization will die tonight, never to be brought back again" if no deal is reached. He also said "maybe something revolutionarily wonderful can happen," keeping the door open for negotiations. Airstrikes intensified with two bridges and a train station hit in Iran.

### Military Developments

- **Airstrikes hit Iranian bridges and train station**: Railway bridge, train station, and highway bridge struck; US/Israel did not immediately claim responsibility (AP News)
- **US strikes Kharg Island again**: White House official confirmed US military hit targets on Iran's oil hub Kharg Island for the second time (AP News)
- **Israel strikes Shiraz petrochemical site for second consecutive day**: Israel said targets were facilities used to make explosives
- **Iran fires 7 ballistic missiles at Saudi Arabia**: Debris rained near energy facilities as they were intercepted
- **Iran fires on Israel**: Incoming missiles reported in Tel Aviv and Eilat
- **IRGC threat**: Iran will "deprive the US and its allies of the region's oil and gas for years" and expand attacks across the Gulf if Trump carries out threat
- **18 killed in Alborz province airstrike**, 15 killed in other strikes (Iranian state media)
- **Khorramabad International Airport struck** (western Iran)

### Diplomatic Developments

- **Iranian president claims 14 million volunteers**: Masoud Pezeshkian posted on X that 14 million answered state media/text message campaigns to volunteer, said he would join
- **IRGC general urges parents to send children to man checkpoints**
- **French FM warns of war crimes**: Jean-Noël Barrot said attacks on civilian/energy infrastructure "are barred by rules of war, international law"
- **UN Secretary-General warns US**: António Guterres warned attacks on civilian infrastructure banned under international law
- **Trump: "not at all" concerned about war crimes**
- **Talks ongoing**: Iran rejected latest US proposal, unclear if deal possible before deadline
