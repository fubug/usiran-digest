## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

**Deadline最后警告（美东4月7日晚8点）**

特朗普设定的伊朗重开霍尔木兹海峡最后期限即将到临。与此同时，以色列向伊朗公众发布安全警告，沙特采取安全措施预防进一步袭击。

### 军事动态

- **以色列警告伊朗人停用火车**：以色列国防军在社交媒体上用波斯语警告伊朗民众，即日起至伊朗时间晚9点（美东下午1:30）不要乘坐火车，称"在火车及铁路线上的存在危及生命"
- **伊朗呼吁形成人链保护发电厂**：最高青年和青少年委员会秘书Alireza Rahimi呼吁青年、运动员、艺术家、学生和教授在发电厂周围形成人链，称发电厂是"国家资产和未来"
- **沙特关闭连接巴林的唯一陆路通道**：沙特阿拉伯在伊朗袭击其东部省份能源设施后，关闭了连接巴林的King Fahd Causeway大桥，全长15.5英里
- **德黑兰犹太教堂被毁**：美以联合空袭完全摧毁了德黑兰的Rafi-Nia犹太教堂，这是伊朗东北呼罗珊犹太人聚集的重要场所
- **商船遇袭**：英国海事行动中心确认，伊朗基什岛附近一艘集装箱船被不明投射物击中，船员安全，无环境报告
- **伊斯坦领馆遇袭**：土耳其媒体报道，以色列领事馆附近发生枪击事件，造成3人死亡，2名警察受伤

### 外交进展

- **伊朗驻巴大使：谈判进入"关键敏感阶段"**：伊朗驻巴大使Reza Amiri Moghadam在X上表示，通过巴勒斯坦调解方进行的战争终止努力正接近"关键、敏感阶段"
- **《华尔街日报》：美伊协议希望渺茫**： citing 高级美国谈判人员和官员称，今晚达成协议的可能性很小
- **特朗普：对可能的战争罪"完全不担心"**：当被问及打击伊朗能源设施是否构成战争罪时，特朗普表示"完全不担心"，称"战争罪是让伊朗拥有核武器"

## English Summary

### Core Events

**Deadline Imminent (April 7, 8:00 PM ET)**

Trump's deadline for Iran to reopen the Strait of Hormuz is approaching. Meanwhile, Israel issued security warnings to the Iranian public, and Saudi Arabia took security precautions against potential attacks.

### Military Developments

- **Israel warns Iranians to avoid trains**: Israel Defense Forces warned Iranian citizens via social media in Persian to refrain from using trains from now until 9 PM Iran time (1:30 PM EDT), stating "Your presence on trains and near railway lines endangers your life"
- **Iran calls for human chains around power plants**: Supreme Council of Youth and Adolescents secretary Alireza Rahimi appealed to "all young people, athletes, artists, students and university students and their professors" to form human chains around power plants, calling them "national assets and capital for Iran's future"
- **Saudi Arabia closes King Fahd Causeway**: Following Iranian missile strikes on its oil-rich Eastern Province, Saudi Arabia closed the only road linking it to Bahrain, the 15.5-mile series of bridges
- **Tehran synagogue destroyed**: U.S.-Israeli strikes "completely destroyed" the Rafi-Nia Synagogue in Tehran, one of the most important gathering places for Khorasan Jews
- **Container vessel attacked**: UK Maritime Trade Operations confirmed a container vessel was struck by an unknown projectile south of Kish Island near the Strait of Hormuz, crew safe
- **Istanbul consulate attack**: Turkish media reports shooting near Israeli consulate, 3 people killed, 2 police officers wounded

### Diplomatic Developments

- **Iran's ambassador: negotiations at "critical, sensitive stage"**: Iranian Ambassador to Pakistan Reza Amiri Moghadam stated on X that efforts to end the war are approaching a "critical, sensitive stage" through Pakistani mediation
- **Wall Street Journal: slim chances of agreement**: Citing senior American negotiators, chances of an agreement by tonight's deadline are slim
- **Trump "not at all concerned about war crimes"**: When asked about potential war crimes from attacking civilian infrastructure, Trump stated he's "not worried about it," claiming "the war crime is allowing Iran to have a nuclear weapon"
