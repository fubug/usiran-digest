## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

特朗普在4月7日晚宣布与伊朗达成为期两周的停火协议，此前他威胁如果伊朗不在东部时间8点前同意重新开放霍尔木兹海峡，将"摧毁一个文明"。

### 军事动态

- - 以色列对伊朗8座桥梁发动空袭，警告民众在当地时间9点前不要乘坐铁路
- - 伊朗中部城市卡尚一座铁路桥被击中，造成至少3人死亡
- - 伊朗民众在桥梁和发电厂周围组成人链，防止可能的空袭
- - 美军在过去的24小时内加大对伊朗的打击力度
- - HRANA记录过去24小时内伊朗16个省份遭遇425次袭击，36名平民死亡，48人受伤

### 外交进展

- - 巴基斯坦成功斡旋，促成两周停火提议
- - 中国在最后一刻呼吁伊朗展现灵活性，缓和紧张局势
- - 伊朗接受巴基斯坦的两周停火方案
- - 伊朗驻巴基斯坦大使表示外交取得"从关键敏感阶段向前迈出一大步"
- - 特朗普强调停火协议"伊斯兰共和国同意立即、完全、安全地重新开放霍尔木兹海峡"

## English Summary

### Core Events

President Trump announced a two-week cease-fire agreement with Iran on the evening of April 7, after threatening to "wipe out a whole civilization" if Iran failed to reopen the Strait of Hormuz by 8 p.m. Eastern time.

### Military Developments

- Israel launched airstrikes on eight bridges across Iran, warning Iranians not to ride railroads until 9 p.m. local time
- At least three people were killed when a railway bridge was hit in Kashan, central Iran
- Iranian citizens formed human chains around bridges and power plants across the country
- United States intensified attacks on Iran over the past 24 hours
- HRANA recorded 425 attacks across 16 provinces in Iran in the past 24 hours, with 36 civilians killed and 48 injured

### Diplomatic Developments

- Pakistan successfully mediated, proposing a two-week cease-fire
- China intervened at the last moment, urging Iran to show flexibility and de-escalate tensions
- Iran accepted Pakistan's two-week cease-fire proposal
- Iran's ambassador to Pakistan stated that diplomacy had "taken a step forward from a critical, sensitive stage"
- Trump emphasized the agreement was "subject to the Islamic Republic of Iran agreeing to the COMPLETE, IMMEDIATE, and SAFE OPENING of the Strait of Hormuz"
