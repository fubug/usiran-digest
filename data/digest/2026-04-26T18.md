## 中文摘要

### 核心动态

**美伊和谈破裂后各方反应**：继17:00特朗普宣布取消特使巴基斯坦和谈行程后，国际社会对美伊局势的新一轮反应正在显现。当前时段（18:00 Beijing Time）显示，美伊双方在和谈破裂后的外交姿态尚未出现明显转变。

### 外交状况

- **美国立场**：特朗普政府表示将继续维持对伊朗的压力政策，包括现有的经济制裁和军事存在
- **伊朗回应**：伊朗官方尚未对特朗普取消和谈行程作出直接公开回应，但通过官方媒体表示拒绝在"不平等条件"下谈判
- **国际反应**：联合国安理会成员对和谈破裂表示关切，呼吁双方保持克制
- **地区影响**：沙特阿拉伯、阿联酋等中东国家重新评估地区安全局势，加强戒备

### 军事态势

- **霍尔木兹海峡**：继续维持紧张状态，美国海军第5舰队保持正常巡逻强度
- **波斯湾**：双方军事部署未见重大调整，但安全警戒级别维持在较高水平
- **国内安全**：伊朗主要城市安全形势总体稳定，正常社会秩序得到维护

### 关键时间点

- 17:00 北京时间：特朗普宣布取消特使巴基斯坦和谈行程
- 17:30 北京时间：伊朗外交部发表简短声明，强调坚持原有谈判立场
- 18:00 北京时间：联合国安理会召开紧急闭门会议讨论局势
- 当日情况：伊朗外长阿卜杜拉希扬已于前日离开巴基斯坦

### 形势评估

美伊和谈的破裂标志着外交解决冲突路径的重大挫折。目前双方立场固化，短期内重启实质性对话的可能性较低。国际社会需要积极介入，推动双方建立新的沟通渠道。军事对抗风险依然存在，但尚未升级到直接冲突程度。预计未来24-48小时内局势将保持相对稳定，但外交层面的僵局可能持续。

## English Summary

### Core Developments

**US-Iran Reactions After Talks Collapse**: Following Trump's announcement at 17:00 Beijing Time canceling the Pakistan peace mission, reactions from the international community are emerging. At the current period (18:00 Beijing Time), both US and Iranian positions show no significant change after the talks breakdown.

### Diplomatic Situation

- **US Position**: The Trump administration indicated it will continue maintaining pressure on Iran, including existing economic sanctions and military presence
- **Iranian Response**: Iranian officials have not made direct public statements regarding Trump's cancellation of the peace mission, but state media emphasized rejection of negotiations "under unequal conditions"
- **International Reaction**: UN Security Council members expressed concern about the talks collapse, calling on both sides to exercise restraint
- **Regional Impact**: Middle Eastern nations including Saudi Arabia and the UAE are reassessing the regional security situation and enhancing preparedness

### Military Posture

- **Strait of Hormuz**: Maintains tense status, with US Navy 5th Fleet maintaining normal patrol intensity
- **Persian Gulf**: No significant adjustments observed in military deployments on both sides, but security alert level remains high
- **Domestic Security**: Major Iranian cities maintain overall stable security situation with normal social order preserved

### Key Timelines

- 17:00 Beijing Time: Trump announces cancellation of envoy Pakistan peace mission
- 17:30 Beijing Time: Iranian Foreign Ministry issues brief statement, emphasizing adherence to original negotiating position
- 18:00 Beijing Time: UN Security Council holds emergency closed-door meeting to discuss the situation
- Daily status: Iranian Foreign Minister Abbas Araghchi left Pakistan the previous day

### Situation Assessment

The collapse of US-Iran peace talks represents a major setback for diplomatic resolution of the conflict. Both sides currently have entrenched positions, making substantive dialogue restart unlikely in the short term. The international community needs to actively intervene to establish new communication channels between the parties. Military confrontation risks remain but have not escalated to direct conflict levels. The situation is expected to remain relatively stable over the next 24-48 hours, though diplomatic deadlock may persist.
