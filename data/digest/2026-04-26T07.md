## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

伊朗明确拒绝与美国直接谈判，除非美国解除对伊朗港口的封锁。美国特使团队仍将按计划前往伊斯兰堡，表明和谈进程进入新的复杂阶段。

### 军事动态

- 以色列在黎巴嫩南部继续进行小规模军事行动，但总体遵守停火协议
- 美军在霍尔木兹海峡继续执行扫雷任务，确保航运安全
- 黎巴嫩真主党保持低强度行动，未违反停火条款
- 黎巴嫩卫生部更新伤亡数据：死亡人数2,496人，受伤人数7,725人

### 外交进展

- **伊朗立场转变**：伊朗外长阿卜杜拉希奇明确表示，除非美国解除对伊朗港口的封锁，否则拒绝直接与美国谈判
- **美国坚持计划**：尽管伊朗拒绝直接谈判，美国特使团队仍将前往伊斯兰堡，继续通过巴基斯坦作为"桥梁"进行谈判
- **和谈机制调整**：美伊和谈从直接接触转向间接通过第三方协调，增加了谈判复杂性
- **地区反应**：海湾盟友向美国提出财政支持请求，显示战争对区域经济的持续影响

### 分析判断

伊朗拒绝直接谈判的声明表明德黑兰在核心利益问题上的强硬立场。美国特使继续行程显示美方希望维持对话渠道，但谈判难度显著增加。通过巴基斯坦作为"桥梁"的间接谈判模式可能延长和谈时间，同时也增加了信息传递的复杂性。黎巴嫩战场的停火相对稳定，但军事存在仍为局势埋下隐患。

## English Summary

### Core Events

Iran has explicitly rejected direct talks with the US unless Washington lifts the blockade on Iranian ports. The US envoy team will proceed to Islamabad as planned, indicating the peace process has entered a new complex phase.

### Military Developments

- Israel continues small-scale military operations in southern Lebanon but generally adheres to the ceasefire agreement
- US continues mine-sweeping operations in the Strait of Hormuz to ensure maritime security
- Hezbollah maintains low-intensity actions without violating ceasefire terms
- Lebanese Health Ministry updated casualty figures: 2,496 dead, 7,725 wounded

### Diplomatic Developments

- **Iran's Position Shift**: Iranian Foreign Minister Abbas Araghchi clearly stated that Iran will not engage in direct talks with the US unless the blockade on Iranian ports is lifted
- **US Proceeds with Plans**: Despite Iran's rejection of direct talks, the US envoy team will still travel to Islamabad, continuing negotiations through Pakistan as a "bridge"
- **Negotiation Mechanism Adjustment**: US-Iran talks have shifted from direct engagement to indirect coordination through third parties, increasing negotiation complexity
- **Regional Response**: Gulf allies have requested financial support from the US, showing the continued economic impact of the conflict

### Analysis

Iran's rejection of direct talks indicates Tehran's hardline stance on core interests. The US proceeding with the envoy mission shows Washington's desire to maintain dialogue channels, but negotiation difficulty has significantly increased. The indirect negotiation model through Pakistan as a "bridge" may prolong the peace process while also increasing complexity in information transmission. The ceasefire in Lebanon remains relatively stable, but military presence still poses potential risks to the situation.
