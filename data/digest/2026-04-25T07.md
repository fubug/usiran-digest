## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

美国特使史蒂夫·威特科夫和贾里德·库什纳将于周六前往伊斯兰堡与伊朗进行新一轮和平会谈，白宫确认。伊朗外长阿卜杜拉希·阿拉格奇已于周五抵达巴基斯坦首都，双方通过巴基斯坦中间人进行间接对话。

### 军事动态

- 以色列在黎巴嫩发动新的空袭，黎巴嫩媒体报道称多个南部城镇遇袭
- 真主党议员明确拒绝特朗普宣布的三周停火延期协议
- 美国国防部表示已拦截34艘伊朗相关船只，封锁范围扩大至全球
- 霍尔木兹海峡航运危机导致巴拿马运河通行费飙升至400万美元

### 外交进展

- 伊朗外长阿拉格奇开启伊斯兰堡、马斯喀特和莫斯科三国访问行程
- 白宫表示伊朗"想要对话"，准备听取伊朗方面的和平方案
- 副总统万斯待命，随时准备前往巴基斯坦参与和谈
- 欧盟外交负责人警告称若没有核专家参与，可能出现比JCPOA更危险的伊朗

## English Summary

### Core Events

U.S. Special Envoy Steve Witkoff and Jared Kushner will travel to Islamabad on Saturday for new peace talks with Iran, the White House confirmed. Iranian Foreign Minister Abbas Araghchi arrived in Pakistan's capital on Friday, with indirect talks mediated by Pakistan.

### Military Developments

- Israel launched new airstrikes in Lebanon, hitting several southern towns, Lebanese media reports
- Hezbollah lawmaker firmly rejects Trump's three-week ceasefire extension
- U.S. Defense Department reports intercepting 34 Iran-linked vessels, expanding blockade globally
- Strait of Hormuz crisis causing Panama Canal transit fees to surge to $4 million

### Diplomatic Developments

- Iranian Foreign Minister Araghchi begins tour of Islamabad, Muscat, and Moscow
- White House states "The Iranians want to talk," ready to hear Iranian peace proposals
- Vice President Vance on standby, prepared to travel to Pakistan if needed
- EU top diplomat warns that without nuclear experts, outcome could be more dangerous than JCPOA
