## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

- 特朗普宣布黎巴嫩-以色列停火延长3周，原定于4月25日到期的停火协议延期
- 伊朗拒绝在美国斡旋下在伊斯兰堡进行第二轮谈判，谴责美国违反停火协议
- 伊朗外交部发言人指出美国自4月13日起对霍尔木兹海峡实施海军封锁，违反停火协议

### 军事动态

- 以色列在黎巴嫩南部建立"黄线"，占领从边境到利塔尼河的区域及邻近海域
- 美国中央司令部称已拦截33艘进出伊朗的船只，继续实施封锁
- 伊朗称夜间扣押了一艘伊朗集装箱船，指责这是对停火协议的违反
- 美国总统特朗普威胁要"射杀"在霍尔木兹海峡铺设水雷的伊朗船只

### 外交进展

- 以色列和黎巴嫩同意10天停火，从4月17日开始，暂停针对黎巴嫩的攻击
- 伊朗外交部声称从停火实施之初就被美国违反，包括海军封锁和扣押船只
- 多个国家正在寻求外交解决方案，美伊僵局持续

## English Summary

### Core Events

- President Trump announced a 3-week extension of the Lebanon-Israel ceasefire, extending the agreement originally set to expire on April 25
- Iran rejected a second round of talks in Islamabad mediated by the US, accusing Washington of violating the ceasefire from the beginning
- Iranian Foreign Ministry spokesman Esmaeil Baghaei stated that the US naval blockade of the Strait of Hormuz since April 13 constituted violations of the truce

### Military Developments

- Israel established a "Yellow Line" in southern Lebanon, occupying the area from the border to the Litani River and the adjacent maritime zone
- US Central Command announced it has intercepted 33 vessels since imposing a blockade on ships coming to and from Iran
- Iran claimed the overnight capture of an Iranian container ship by the US military was a breach of the truce and international law
- President Trump threatened to "shoot and kill" Iranian vessels laying mines in the Strait of Hormuz

### Diplomatic Developments

- Israel and Lebanon agreed on a 10-day ceasefire starting April 17, halting Israeli attacks which have killed nearly 2,300 people
- Iranian authorities stated that Washington violated the ceasefire from its implementation, citing naval blockade and ship seizures
- Multiple countries are seeking diplomatic solutions as the US-Iran stalemate continues
