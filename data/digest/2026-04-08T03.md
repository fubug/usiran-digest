### 核心事件

- **美以协同打击升级**：美军第二次打击伊朗关键石油出口枢纽哈尔克岛，以色列摧毁伊朗8座桥梁

- **黎巴嫩伤亡惨重**：以军在黎巴嫩与真主党交战已造成1,497人死亡
- **人道主义救援**：美国记者雪莉·基特尔森被伊拉克亲伊朗民兵释放

### 外交进展

- **联合国行动失败**：俄罗斯和中国否决了旨在重新开放霍尔木兹海峡的联合国安理会决议
- **国际社会谴责**：联合国秘书长古特雷斯警告任何军事目标都不足以成为摧毁社会基础设施的理由；教皇利奥十四世称特朗普的威胁"完全不可接受"
- **国内政治反应**：美国众议院民主党领袖联合声明要求立即召回议员，称特朗普"完全失去理智"，要求投票结束这场"鲁莽的战争选择"

## English Summary

### Core Events

President Trump issued a devastating threat that "a whole civilization will die tonight," demanding Iran reopen the Strait of Hormuz by 8 p.m. Eastern time or face destruction of all Iranian bridges and power plants. This threat has sparked international condemnation, including from the UN, Vatican, and multiple world leaders.

### Military Developments

- **US-Israeli Escalation**: US forces struck Iran's critical oil export hub of Kharg Island for the second time, while Israel destroyed 8 bridges in Iran
- **Iranian Missile Response**: Iran fired its 7th missile barrage at Israel, targeting southern regions, Tel Aviv, and the occupied West Bank
- **Lebanon Casualties**: Israeli operations against Hezbollah in Lebanon have resulted in 1,497 deaths
- **Humanitarian Development**: American journalist Shelly Kittleson was released by an Iran-backed Iraqi militia

### Diplomatic Developments

- **UN Action Failed**: Russia and China vetoed a UN Security Council resolution aimed at reopening the Strait of Hormuz
- **International Condemnation**: UN Secretary-General Guterres warned that no military objective justifies the wholesale destruction of civilian infrastructure; Pope Leo XIV called Trump's threat "truly unacceptable"
- **Domestic Political Response**: House Democratic leaders issued a joint statement demanding immediate recall of lawmakers, calling Trump "completely unhinged" and demanding votes to end this "reckless war of choice"
