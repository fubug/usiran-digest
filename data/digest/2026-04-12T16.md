## 中文摘要

### 核心事件

美伊伊斯兰堡和谈破裂，特朗普宣布美国海军将对霍尔木兹海峡实施封锁，威胁对伊朗船只使用武力。高层会谈结束未达成协议，美国与伊朗在核计划、海峡控制等问题上分歧严重。

### 军事动态

- 特朗普宣布美国海军将对霍尔木兹海峡实施"完全封锁"，拒绝任何例外

- 以色列与黎巴嫩驻美大使下周将举行罕见直接谈判

### 外交进展

- 美伊伊斯兰堡马拉松和谈结束，双方在核计划和海峡控制等核心问题上未达成协议
- 特朗普指责伊朗"拒绝接受美国条款"，伊朗谈判代表称美国"未能获得代表团信任"
- 巴基斯坦总理称和谈陷入"僵局"，但表示"和谈并未死亡"
- 多国领导人呼吁继续谈判，包括阿曼、欧盟、俄罗斯、英国、澳大利亚
- 教皇利奥十四世呼吁黎巴嫩停火，尊重国际法

## English Summary

### Core Events

U.S.-Iran peace talks in Islamabad collapsed after marathon sessions lasting over 9 hours, with President Trump announcing a U.S. naval blockade of the Strait of Hormuz. The talks failed to resolve key differences over Iran's nuclear program and control of the strategic waterway.

### Military Developments

- Trump announced a "complete blockade" of the Strait of Hormuz with no exceptions for allies
- U.S., UK and other countries are sending minesweepers to clear Iranian-laid mines
- Israeli airstrike killed 6 civilians in southern Lebanese town of Maaroub
- Saudi Arabia restored East-West oil pipeline to full capacity (7 million barrels per day)
- Israeli and Lebanese ambassadors to U.S. will hold rare direct talks next week

### Diplomatic Developments

- U.S.-Iran talks ended without breakthrough, with both sides blaming each other
- Trump accused Iran of "choosing not to accept our terms"
- Iranian negotiator said U.S. was "unable to gain trust of Iranian delegation"
- Pakistani PM called it a "stalemate" but noted "talks are not dead"
- Multiple leaders urged continued negotiations including Oman, EU, Russia, UK, Australia
- Pope Leo XIV called for ceasefire in Lebanon and respect of international law
